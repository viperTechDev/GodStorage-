📖 HOW IT WORKS

Your file's bytes are stored in the Red, Green, and Blue channels of PNG pixels. PNG is lossless — every byte is recovered perfectly. With camouflage, your data hides in the least significant bits of a real photo, making it visually indistinguishable from the original.
